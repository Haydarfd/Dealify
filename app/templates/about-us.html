About Us

Welcome to Dealify, the UAE’s premier marketplace where simplicity meets security. Whether you're buying or selling, we’re here to make every transaction effortless and rewarding.

Who We Are

At Dealify, we’re more than just a platform – we’re a growing community of buyers and sellers built on trust. Our goal is to empower individuals and businesses with a secure space to connect, trade, and thrive with confidence.

What we offer

Dealify covers everything someone might think of: cars, electronics, fashion, properties, services, and more — all in one easy-to-use platform.

Our Mission

For Sellers: An easy-to-use platform to showcase your products and services with maximum visibility.

For Buyers: A variety of verified listings and personalized experiences.

For Everyone: A safe marketplace where trust is at the heart of every transaction.

Why Do We Charge for Listings?

We use a unique credit-based system. Our packages start from AED 10, which includes 1 credit and allows you to post 2 listings. One listing = 0.5 credit for most categories, while others vary (e.g., Cars: 6 credits, Properties: 12 credits). Buy more credits at once and save — check the Packages page for more info
